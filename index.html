Stop Believing These AI Myths

Misconceptions about AI are the biggest barrier to adoption. Here are three that might be holding you back.

Myth

"AI will replace my staff"

AI replaces tasks, not people. Your team spends hours on repetitive work that adds no value. Automation frees them to do the work that actually grows your business.

Myth

"It's too expensive for a small business"

The ROI on automation is typically measured in weeks, not years. A single automated process can save hundreds of hours annually. See the real numbers.

Myth

"We're not ready yet"

If you have processes and people, you're ready. You don't need perfect data or a tech team. Take our readiness checklist.
