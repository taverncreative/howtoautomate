AI Consultancy for UK Small Businesses

We shadow your team, document your processes, and identify exactly what to automate. No guesswork. No jargon. Just a structured approach to AI adoption that actually works.

The Problem: You Know AI Matters, but You Do Not Know Where to Start

Every week, you hear about another business using AI to save time, cut costs, or grow faster. You know your business should be doing the same — but the gap between knowing and doing feels enormous.

Perhaps you have tried a few AI tools and found them underwhelming. Perhaps you have spoken to technology vendors who seem more interested in selling their platform than understanding your business. Or perhaps you simply cannot work out which of your dozens of processes should be automated first.

The result is always the same: nothing changes. Your team keeps doing things manually. Your competitors pull further ahead. And the gap between where you are and where you could be grows wider every month.

That is exactly the problem our process mapping and AI consultancy service solves. We do not start with technology. We start with your business.

What Is Included

A comprehensive, hands-on engagement that covers every step from understanding your business to implementing real automation.

Process Shadowing

We sit with your team and observe how work actually gets done — not how it is supposed to get done. We watch the workarounds, the bottlenecks, and the steps that eat up time.

  • On-site observation of daily workflows
  • Interviews with team members at every level
  • Identification of hidden inefficiencies
  • Documentation of actual vs. intended processes

SOP Development

We turn our observations into clear, complete standard operating procedures. These SOPs become the foundation for automation — and a valuable resource for your business regardless.

  • Step-by-step process documentation
  • Process flow diagrams and visual maps
  • Role and responsibility matrices
  • Decision trees for complex workflows

Automation Strategy

With documented processes in hand, we identify the highest-impact automation opportunities. You will see exactly what we recommend, why, and what the expected return looks like.

  • Prioritised automation opportunity register
  • Cost-benefit analysis for each opportunity
  • Technology recommendations and tool selection
  • Phased implementation roadmap

Training & Ongoing Support

We do not just hand over a document and walk away. We implement the automations, train your team, and provide ongoing support to ensure everything works as expected.

  • Hands-on implementation of automations
  • Staff training sessions and documentation
  • Post-launch monitoring and adjustment
  • Optional ongoing support packages

How It Works

A structured, six-step process that takes you from initial conversation to fully implemented automation.

Discovery Call

A free, no-obligation conversation where we learn about your business, your pain points, and your goals. We will tell you honestly whether our consultancy is the right fit — and if not, where else to look. Book your free call today.

Process Shadowing

We spend time on-site (or remotely) with your team, observing how work gets done across every department. We watch, ask questions, and document everything. This is where the real insights emerge.

SOP Documentation

We compile our observations into comprehensive standard operating procedures. These SOPs map your processes step by step, identify bottlenecks, and highlight automation opportunities. Learn more about our process mapping service.

Automation Design

We design the automation strategy: which processes to automate first, which tools to use, and how the changes will be rolled out. You get a clear roadmap with timelines and expected ROI.

Implementation

We build and deploy the automations — whether that means configuring existing tools, connecting your systems via SOP automation, or developing custom software. Everything is tested thoroughly before going live.

Training & Handover

We train your team on every new system and process. You receive full documentation, and we provide a support period to iron out any issues. Your team will be confident and self-sufficient.

Who This Is For

Our AI consultancy service is designed for UK small and medium-sized businesses that meet most of these criteria:

  • You have 5 or more staff performing repeatable processes
  • Your team spends significant time on manual, repetitive tasks
  • You know AI could help but do not know where to start
  • You do not have an internal tech team or IT department
  • You want a practical, honest approach — not a sales pitch
  • You are ready to invest in improving how your business operates

We work across a range of industries including retail, hospitality, professional services, construction, healthcare, and manufacturing. If your business has processes, we can help automate them.

Frequently Asked Questions

Honest answers to the questions we hear most often.

How long does the AI consultancy process take?

A typical engagement runs between 4 and 12 weeks depending on the size and complexity of your business. The discovery and shadowing phase usually takes 1–2 weeks, followed by documentation and strategy design, then implementation. We will give you a clear timeline after the initial discovery call.

Do we need a tech team or IT department?

No. We work with businesses that have no internal tech team at all. That is actually our sweet spot. We handle the technical side entirely and train your existing staff to use and maintain the new systems. You do not need any technical knowledge to work with us.

What if we are not ready for AI?

If you have processes and people, you are ready. You do not need perfect data, a tech team, or previous experience with automation. Our consultancy starts with understanding where you are now and building a practical path forward. Take our AI readiness checklist if you want to assess your current position.

How much does AI consultancy cost?

Every engagement is different, so we do not publish fixed prices. We will give you a clear, detailed quote after the free discovery call once we understand your needs. There are no hidden fees and no ongoing commitments unless you choose ongoing support. Read our guide on the ROI of automation to understand the typical returns.

Will AI replace our staff?

No. AI replaces tasks, not people. The goal is to free your team from repetitive, low-value work so they can focus on the things that actually grow your business — like serving customers, solving problems, and building relationships. Most clients find their staff are happier after automation because the boring parts of their jobs disappear.

What industries do you work with?

We work with UK SMEs across retail, hospitality, professional services, construction, healthcare, and manufacturing. However, our approach works for any business with repeatable processes, regardless of industry.

What happens after the consultancy is finished?

You will have documented SOPs, a clear automation strategy, and implemented automations with your team trained to use them. We offer optional ongoing support packages, but there is no obligation. Many clients then move into our SOP automation or custom software development services as they scale.
