Automate Your SOPs with AI

Turn your documented processes into automated workflows. Reduce errors, save time, and free your team for work that actually needs a human.

The Problem: You Have SOPs but People Still Do Them Manually

You have done the right thing. Your processes are documented. Your team has standard operating procedures to follow. But here is the uncomfortable truth: having SOPs and actually automating them are two very different things.

Every day, your team follows documented steps that a computer could handle faster, more accurately, and without getting tired. They copy data from one system to another. They send the same email notifications manually. They generate reports by pulling numbers from three different spreadsheets. They chase approvals through email threads.

These are not complex tasks. They are repetitive, predictable, and rule-based — which makes them perfect for automation. Every hour your team spends on work like this is an hour they are not spending on the things that actually grow your business: serving customers, solving problems, and making decisions.

If you are interested in understanding the financial impact, our guide on the ROI of automation breaks down the real numbers. The results are often startling.

What Gets Automated

These are the types of processes we automate most frequently. If your team does any of these manually, you are leaving time and money on the table.

Data Entry & Transfer

Automatically move data between systems. When a new order comes in, it gets entered into your CRM, accounting software, and stock system simultaneously — without anyone touching a keyboard.

Email & Notification Sequences

Automated emails and notifications triggered by events in your business. Client onboarding sequences, order confirmations, payment reminders, internal alerts — all sent at the right time to the right people.

Report Generation

Automated reports generated on schedule or on demand. Pull data from multiple sources, format it to your specifications, and deliver it to the right people — daily, weekly, or monthly.

Approval Workflows

Structured approval processes with automatic routing, reminders, and escalation. Purchase approvals, leave requests, document sign-offs — no more chasing people through email or messaging apps.

Invoice Processing

Automated invoice creation, sending, and reconciliation. When a job is completed or an order is fulfilled, the invoice is generated and sent automatically. Payment tracking and reminders follow without manual intervention.

Scheduling & Reminders

Automated appointment scheduling, task assignments, and deadline reminders. Your team stays on track without anyone needing to manage calendars or chase follow-ups manually.

How It Works

A structured, seven-step process from reviewing your existing SOPs to fully automated, monitored workflows.

SOP Review

We review your existing standard operating procedures to understand each process in detail — inputs, outputs, decision points, and dependencies. If your SOPs need updating first, our process mapping service can help.

Automation Audit

We assess each SOP for automation potential. We identify which steps can be fully automated, which need partial automation with human oversight, and which should remain manual. You get a clear picture of what is possible and what the return will be.

Tool Selection

We recommend the right tools for each automation based on your needs, budget, and existing tech stack. We are tool-agnostic — we always choose the best fit, not our favourite platform.

Build & Configure

We build the automated workflows, connecting your tools, setting up triggers, and configuring every step to match your SOP exactly. Each automation is built to handle edge cases and errors gracefully.

Testing

We test every automation thoroughly with real data, checking for edge cases, error handling, and performance. Nothing goes live until it has been tested and you have approved it.

Go Live

We deploy the automations, train your team on any changes to their workflow, and monitor the first runs to ensure everything works as expected in production. Get in touch to start the process.

Monitor & Optimise

We monitor the automations over time, fix any issues, and optimise for better performance. As your processes evolve, we update the automations to match. Your workflows get better over time, not worse.

Who This Is For

SOP automation is ideal for businesses that have documented processes (or are willing to document them first) and want to stop doing them manually. This service works particularly well for:

  • Businesses with documented SOPs that are still executed manually
  • Teams spending hours per week on data entry, reporting, or notifications
  • Organisations that have already been through process mapping
  • Companies using multiple software tools that do not talk to each other
  • Businesses that want quick wins before committing to larger automation projects
  • Teams frustrated by repetitive tasks that add no value to the business

If your processes are not documented yet, we recommend starting with our process mapping service or a full AI consultancy engagement. Alternatively, if your automation needs require entirely new tools, our custom software development service may be the better fit.

Frequently Asked Questions

What you need to know about automating your standard operating procedures.

Do we need documented SOPs before we start?

Ideally yes. Documented SOPs make automation faster and more accurate because we know exactly what to build. If your processes are not documented yet, we recommend starting with our process mapping service first. However, for simple, well-understood processes, we can document and automate them together.

What tools do you use for automation?

We are tool-agnostic and choose the best platform for each specific automation. This might include tools like Make, Zapier, Power Automate, n8n, or custom-built solutions depending on the complexity, volume, and your existing tech stack. We always recommend the most cost-effective and reliable option.

How quickly will we see results?

Most automations start delivering time savings from the moment they go live. A typical SOP automation project takes 2 to 8 weeks from SOP review to go-live depending on the number and complexity of processes. Simple automations like data entry or email notifications can be live within days.

What if an automation breaks?

Every automation we build includes error handling and monitoring. If something goes wrong, the system alerts us and we fix it promptly. We also build fallback procedures so your business is never stuck. Our optional support packages provide ongoing monitoring and maintenance.

Can you automate processes that involve multiple systems?

Yes — that is one of the most common scenarios. We connect multiple systems so data flows automatically between them. For example, an order placed in your CRM can automatically create an invoice in your accounting software, send a confirmation email, and update your stock levels — all without manual intervention.

What is the difference between SOP automation and custom software development?

SOP automation uses existing tools and platforms to automate your documented processes. It is typically faster and more cost-effective. Custom software development involves building entirely new applications from scratch. We recommend SOP automation when existing tools can handle your needs, and custom development when they cannot.
