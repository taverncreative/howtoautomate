AI Automation for UK Hospitality Businesses

Reduce no-shows, simplify rota planning, and take the admin out of running a restaurant, pub, hotel, or cafe.

Common Challenges in Hospitality

Hospitality is one of the most operationally intense industries. These are the time-drains we help eliminate.

No-Shows

Empty tables on a Friday night cost more than just the covers — you've already prepped food, scheduled staff, and turned away other bookings. Without automated reminders, no-show rates of 15-20% are common.

Complex Rota Scheduling

Balancing staff availability, shift preferences, holiday requests, and fluctuating demand is a weekly headache. Most managers spend 3-4 hours building rotas that still end up with gaps or overstaffing.

Manual Supplier Ordering

Ordering from multiple suppliers means phone calls, WhatsApp messages, and handwritten lists. It's easy to over-order perishables or run out of key ingredients mid-service.

Inconsistent Guest Communication

Following up on reviews, sending post-visit thank-yous, and promoting special events all fall by the wayside when the team is focused on service. Opportunities to build loyalty slip through the cracks.

Time-Consuming Cash-Up

End-of-night reconciliation means counting tills, matching card payments, checking tips, and preparing the bank deposit. It adds 45 minutes to an already long day, and errors are common when staff are tired.

Hospitality Automation FAQ

Can automation really reduce no-shows at my restaurant?

Yes. Automated booking confirmation and reminder sequences — typically an email at booking, an SMS 24 hours before, and a final reminder 2 hours before — reduce no-shows by 30-50% on average. Some of our clients also add a simple cancellation link that frees the table for walk-ins, which recovers revenue that would otherwise be lost entirely.

How does automated rota scheduling work?

The system analyses your booking volume, historical covers data, and seasonal patterns to suggest optimal staffing levels for each shift. Your manager still approves the final rota, but the heavy lifting of matching availability to demand is handled automatically. Most managers tell us it cuts rota-building time by 80%.

Will my kitchen team need technical skills to use these automations?

Not at all. We design our hospitality automations to work in the background or through simple interfaces like tablets and phones. If your team can use a booking app, they can use our tools. We also provide hands-on training as part of every engagement, and we stay available for questions after go-live.

Can you automate allergen tracking and compliance?

Absolutely. We can link your menu items to allergen databases so that any menu change automatically flags allergen implications. This helps you stay compliant with Natasha's Law and gives your front-of-house team instant access to accurate allergen information when guests ask.

How quickly can we get started with hospitality automation?

Most hospitality projects start with a one-week discovery phase, followed by 2-4 weeks of build. Simple automations like booking reminders can be live within a fortnight. More complex integrations with EPOS and supplier systems typically take 4-6 weeks end to end.
