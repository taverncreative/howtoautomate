AI for Accountants, Solicitors & Consultants

Automate the admin that stops you doing billable work. From client onboarding to regulatory deadlines, we help professional services firms reclaim their time.

Common Challenges in Professional Services

Your expertise is what clients pay for, yet too much of your day is consumed by admin. These are the issues we solve.

Slow Client Onboarding

Chasing documents, sending engagement letters, running AML checks, and setting up new clients in your practice management system can take days. Every delay risks losing the client to a competitor who responds faster.

Manual Time Tracking

Reconstructing timesheets at the end of the week from memory and calendar entries means you're almost certainly under-recording billable hours. Most professionals lose 10-15% of recoverable time through poor tracking.

Regulatory Deadline Pressure

HMRC deadlines, Companies House filings, court dates, and professional body requirements create a constant background hum of anxiety. Miss one and the consequences range from fines to reputational damage.

Document Versioning Chaos

When multiple team members work on the same documents, version control becomes a nightmare. Emails with attachments named "FINAL_v3_ACTUAL_FINAL" are still depressingly common in professional services.

Inconsistent Billing

Invoices go out late, time entries are missed, and work in progress builds up because nobody has time to review and bill it. The result: cash flow problems that are entirely preventable.

Professional Services Automation FAQ

Is automated client onboarding secure enough for professional services?

Yes. We build onboarding workflows with encrypted file uploads, secure document storage, and access controls that meet the requirements of the SRA, ICAEW, and ACCA. All data stays within UK-hosted infrastructure unless you specify otherwise. We can also build in audit trails for compliance purposes.

Can automation integrate with practice management software like Xero or Clio?

Absolutely. We integrate with all major practice management, accounting, and case management platforms including Xero, QuickBooks, Clio, PracticePanther, and IRIS. Our automations sit alongside your existing tools rather than replacing them, which means minimal disruption to your current workflows.

How do you handle regulatory compliance in automated workflows?

We build compliance checks directly into the workflow. For example, AML verification steps are embedded in client onboarding, and regulatory deadline tracking is automated with escalation alerts. We work with you to ensure every automation meets your professional body's requirements and includes appropriate audit trails.

What's the typical time saving for a small accountancy practice?

A typical 5-15 person practice saves 15-25 hours per week through automation of client onboarding, time recording, billing, and deadline tracking. The biggest single saving usually comes from automated document collection during onboarding, which alone can save 3-5 hours per new client.

Can AI help with document drafting for solicitors?

Yes, within appropriate limits. AI can generate first drafts of standard documents like engagement letters, terms of business, and template correspondence. A qualified professional always reviews and approves the final version. This typically reduces drafting time by 60-70% while maintaining the quality and accuracy your clients expect.

How long does it take to implement automation in a professional services firm?

Most implementations take 4-8 weeks from initial consultation to go-live. We start with a process mapping phase to understand your specific workflows, then build and test automations in stages. Client onboarding automation is usually the quickest win, often live within 2-3 weeks.
